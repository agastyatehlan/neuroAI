RAISED BY THE INTERNET

A Generation Growing Up Online

How the Internet Affects Mental Capacity

Constant exposure to short-form content and rapid digital interaction has shaped how Gen Z processes information. Attention spans can shorten due to continuous scrolling and multitasking between apps. Deep focus becomes harder, but fast information scanning and digital navigation skills improve significantly.

How the Internet Benefits Gen Z

The internet provides unlimited access to knowledge, skill-building platforms, global networking, and AI tools. Young individuals can create businesses, build personal brands, and learn anything instantly. It expands opportunity far beyond traditional boundaries.
